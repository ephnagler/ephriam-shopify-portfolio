## Challenge

While Shopify's native Buy X Get Y discounts are a powerful tool, their visual and functional customization options are limited. I needed a dynamic, visually engaging solution that could respond in real-time to cart contents.

## Solution

I developed a custom Shopify application that provides a wide range of visually compelling and highly customizable promotion displays. The app dynamically represents the Buy X Get Y promotion as items are added to the cart, enhancing the user experience and encouraging conversions.

### Key Features

- Qualified products section (configurable for any page or cart).
- Custom-built price snippets and product cards for collections.
- Dynamic pop-up modals, toast notifications, and sticky banners.
- Floating button and side-panel displays.
- Progress reward bar to gamify cart value.
